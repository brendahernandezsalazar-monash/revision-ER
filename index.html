The City Is About To Be Taken

It is 1926. The city is divided between rival underground families. Your group, the , has learned that a competing gang is about to seize control using a carefully coordinated operation. The details of the plot have been split across four protected locations so that no single informant can betray the full plan.

Your team must search the city, decode hidden evidence, and recover the four fragments before the rival gang closes in. Every clue is concealed within anatomical puzzles, physiological consequences, and coded case files. Solve the leads, assemble the fragments, and uncover the final plan before time runs out.
