Asylum Protocols: A Guide for the Uninitiated

Read these instructions carefully. Your survival depends on it.

Free the Spirit

Your primary objective is to solve a series of anatomical puzzles within 30 minutes to find the key that unlocks the anatomist's chains. Each solved puzzle brings you one step closer to setting his spirit free and escaping this cursed asylum.

Navigate the asylum using the map, investigate medical records, and submit your answers to progress. Be quick, for the spirit's rage grows with every passing minute.

Interacting with Your Environment

  • Text Input: Type your answer (the letter or the number) here. Case does not matter.
  • Submit Answer: Click this to check your text-based answer.
  • No Hints: In this unforgiving place, you are on your own. No hints are available.
  • Asylum Map: Your guide through the asylum. Blue nodes are unlocked puzzles, green are solved, and grey are still locked. Click a blue node to begin.

The Price of Failure

  • Starting Sanity: You begin with 250 points. Guard them well.
  • Correct Answer: Solving a puzzle earns you +100 points.
  • Incorrect Answer: Each mistake enrages the spirit, costing you a severe -100 points.
  • No Hints: You will find no help in these halls.
  • Time Bonus: Escape quickly to earn a bonus based on your remaining time.

If your score reaches zero, your mind is lost to the asylum forever. The game ends.
